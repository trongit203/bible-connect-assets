‚ùìFrequently Asked Questions

How do I save a Bible verse?

While reading a chapter, tap the bookmark icon next to any verse you'd like to save. Access your saved verses anytime from the Bible tab.

Can I use the app without an account?

Yes! You can browse the Bible as a guest. However, to create prayers, join the community, and use the AI companion, you'll need to sign up for a free account.

How do I share a prayer with the community?

When creating a new prayer, make sure "Community" is selected. Your prayer will be visible to other users who can react and support you in your faith journey.

What does "Amen" a prayer mean?

"Amen" is a way to show support and agreement with someone's prayer. It's like saying "I'm praying with you" or "I agree with this prayer."

How does the AI Faith Companion work?

Our AI companion is trained to provide biblical guidance, answer faith questions, and offer encouragement. It's like having a knowledgeable friend to discuss spiritual matters with.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes! Your personal prayers are completely private unless you choose to share them with the community. We take your privacy seriously and protect your data with industry-standard security.

Can I comment on prayers?

Yes! Tap the comment icon on any prayer to add encouraging words, share your thoughts, or offer support to fellow believers.

How do I change my prayer categories?

When creating a prayer, you can choose from categories like Gratitude, Healing, Guidance, Peace, Strength, or Other. Each category has its own color to help organize your prayers.

üîßTroubleshooting

App not loading?

  • Check your internet connection
  • Force close and restart the app
  • Make sure you have the latest version installed
  • Clear the app cache in your device settings

Can't sign in?

  • Verify your email and password are correct
  • Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" option
  • Check if your account email has been verified

Prayers not syncing?

  • Ensure you're connected to the internet
  • Pull down to refresh the prayer list
  • Sign out and sign back in to refresh your session

Push notifications not working?

  • Check notification permissions in your device settings
  • Enable notifications in the app's Profile/Settings
  • Restart your device
